Tracker's Tavern Stat Sheets

Welcome to the Tracker Tavern!
Your place to learn more about the threats you may encounter outside of (and occasionally within) Avalon's city walls!
Our professional monster trackers have accumulated a library of fact sheets for you to take on your adventures. Each sheet costs 10gp, and for every 3 sheets you buy, you get a fourth as a gift!
All funds raised will be used towards our tracking and pet training efforts.
