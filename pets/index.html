Hello! and welcome to the basic pet training pet training guide, as written by me, Ramit110. This page is for new pet owners and only covers the bare basics of what is needed as well as particularly useful skills for pets to have. It is reccomended to read through the Avalon Pet Rules document before reading this or any other guides on this page.

Core Pet Training Skills

  • Move
  • Stay
  • Come
  • Calm - This and the three commands above are the "Basic Commands", before this point your pet is not considered trained and will not be commandable in Combat or out of it.
  • Forage - If you like materials, for money or crafting then you need This skill.
  • Dig - even if if your pet can fly this is a useful skill in and out of combat.
  • Distant Messenger - if you have the prerequisites for this, the feat is incredibly useful.

Combat Pet Training Guide

  • Persistent Spirit - anyone who can should train this skill as a replacement for the Hardy skill. Advantage on int, wis or cha saving throws can be a great benefit to anyone involved.
  • Hardy - this is the single most important skill for any combat readied pet, bonus hp, advantage on str, dex or con saving throws and the ability to use death saves.
  • Mobile Pet - giving a pet the reaction skill to move up to your speed can help in a myriad of different scenarios.
  • Personal Porter - A life saving skill for any scenario where emergency healing is needed.
